Correlates of Personality & Behavior

The Dark Triad & Cluster B Traits

Research consistently links certain personality structures with higher rates of unethical and harmful relational behaviors. The "Dark Triad" (Narcissism, Machiavellianism, Psychopathy) and Cluster B Personality Disorders (BPD, NPD) are significant predictors.

  • Higher Infidelity: Traits are strongly correlated with a greater likelihood of cheating.
  • Manipulation & Control: Machiavellianism, in particular, predicts strategic manipulation.
  • Emotional Instability: BPD is associated with intense, unstable relationships and frantic efforts to avoid abandonment.
